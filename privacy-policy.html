Privacy Policy — FCD Driver

Last updated: June 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how First Class Drive ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the FCD Driver mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide our transportation management services:

  • Account information: username and encrypted password to authenticate drivers.
  • Location data: GPS location is collected while the driver is on an active trip to enable real-time tracking and automatic mileage calculation.
  • Trip data: trip details including pickup/drop-off addresses, trip status, and timestamps.
  • Device information: basic device data needed to operate the app correctly.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To assign and manage transportation trips.
  • To track real-time driver location during active trips.
  • To calculate trip mileage and duration.
  • To communicate trip assignments and updates to drivers.
  • To generate trip reports for operational purposes.

3. Location Data

The App requests access to your device's GPS location. Location data is only collected while you are logged in and have an active trip. Location information is shared with the First Class Drive administrator to monitor trip progress. We do not sell or share your location data with third parties.

4. Data Storage

All data is stored securely using Google Firebase Realtime Database. Data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS protocols. We retain trip data for operational and reporting purposes.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • With the First Class Drive administrative team for operational purposes.
  • When required by law or legal process.
  • With Google Firebase as our data storage provider (subject to Google's privacy policy).

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures including password hashing (SHA-256) and encrypted data transmission to protect your information against unauthorized access.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account data.

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

8. Children's Privacy

The FCD Driver app is intended for use by professional drivers only and is not directed to children under the age of 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the app or by email.

11. Account Deletion

You may request the deletion of your FCD Driver account and all associated personal data at any time. To submit a deletion request, please contact us at:

Email: admin@firstclassdrive.com
Subject: Account Deletion Request

Please include your driver username in the request. We will process your request and permanently delete your account data (including location history, trip records, and login credentials) within 30 days. Note that some data may be retained if required by law or for legitimate operational purposes.
